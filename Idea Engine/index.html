Brainstorming

Start here, write down anything that comes to your mind. Colors, websites you like, questions, designs, short-term goals any particular audiences, products, tools, solutions, etc.

Idea Screening

Pros and Cons. Allow someone you trust to help widdle. Talk to a TA. A MECE list may help here as well.The MECE principle, pronounced "ME-see", is a grouping principle for separating a set of items into subsets that are mutually exclusive (ME) and collectively exhaustive (CE).

Inspiration

Ask yourself... Why? Finding what motivates you for your project idea will make it both enjoyable and rewarding. Having PASSION is key.

What is the Problem

Analyze the SPECIFIC problem your audience faces and Develop your approach to solving that problem.

Ask Around

See what your peers, family, and the internet think about your project.

Pinpoint Audience

Your SPECIFIC customer, the problem they face, and your solution for that problem should be set it stone. Aks the questions, is this a realistic problem, is my SPA solving their specific problem, can i be more specific?

Aspirations

Your goals for project, what functionality you want in your SPA. Bring back some love from your brainstorming session. Add personality, in our opinion, this is where you become a CSS nerd.

Framework

The looks of the operation is critical. You want individuality, add the spice, trust us! Check out the Great Hall for Resources to help build your framework.

Clean Up & Testing

This part of your project wont happen until the last four weeks, be sure to keep up with your Kanban, Trello, or Jira boards! Use the resources to brush up your look, YOUR DEV TOOLS are a BIG help!
