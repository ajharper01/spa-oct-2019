Daydreams

Average occurrence of 70-120 minutes a day. Classified as a level of consciousness between sleep and wakefulness.

Lucid Dreams

Being "awake" while dreaming. Some can remain in the lucid state of dreaming and make decisions in their dreams influencing the dreamâ€™s outcome without awakening.

Nightmares

Causes the dreamer to wake up feeling anxious and frightened maybe from a response to real life trauma. An indication of a fear that needs to be acknowledged and confronted. NOTE: This type of nightmare falls under a special category called Post-traumatic Stress Nightmare (PSN).

Recurring Dreams

Repeat themselves because a conflict depicted in the dream remains unresolved or ignored.

Healing Dreams

Messages for the dreamer regarding their or a loved one's health.

Prophetic Dreams

Precognitive or psychic dreams that seem to foretell the future.

Signal Dreams

Signal dreams help you solve problems or make decisions in your waking life.

Epic Dreams

Epic dreams (or Great dreams) are so compelling, and so vivid that you cannot ignore them. These dreams possess much beauty and contain many archetypal symbology.

Aspirations

Personal Dreams (ideas or goals you have) are the other side of this coin. These can be something simple or something huge. Either way they can have a big impact on the world we all live in.
